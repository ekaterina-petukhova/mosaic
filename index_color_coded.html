Mosaic Certificate of Performance

Proprietary Efficacy
Certification.

The Mosaic CoP is a rigorous, data-backed performance document issued after 3D biological testing on our standardized skin model. It is not an official EU regulatory approval — it is a proprietary scientific evaluation designed to support claim substantiation and R&D decision-making.

📊

What It Measures

Quantitative readouts across up to 8 biological endpoints: hydration markers, barrier function, collagen stimulation, inflammatory response, proliferation, oxidative stress, and more.

🔗

Claim Substantiation

The report is structured to support the evidential requirements for cosmetic claims under EU Commission Regulation (EU) No 655/2013 — while remaining entirely distinct from regulatory approval.

🛡️

What It Is Not

The Mosaic CoP is not an official EU certification or pre-market regulatory clearance. It is a proprietary scientific evaluation issued by Mosaic S.r.l. based on internal validated methods.
